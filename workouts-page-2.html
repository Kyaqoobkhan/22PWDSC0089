Lower Body Strength

This workout targets your lower body, building strength and endurance.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps (each leg)
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15 reps

HIIT Workout

This High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workout maximizes calorie burn in a short time.

Exercises:

  • Burpees: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds rest
  • Jump Squats: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds rest
  • Push-Ups: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds rest
  • High Knees: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds rest
