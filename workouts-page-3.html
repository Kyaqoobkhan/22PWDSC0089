Flexibility & Mobility

This routine focuses on improving your flexibility and mobility through stretching exercises.

Exercises:

  • Hamstring Stretch: 3 sets of 30 seconds each leg
  • Quadriceps Stretch: 3 sets of 30 seconds each leg
  • Shoulder Stretch: 3 sets of 30 seconds each arm
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 3 sets of 10 reps
