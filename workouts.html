Full Body Workout

This full-body workout targets all major muscle groups, helping you build strength and endurance.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Remember to warm up before starting and cool down afterward.

Cardio Blast

This cardio workout is perfect for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health.

Exercises:

  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Stay hydrated and keep your intensity high!
